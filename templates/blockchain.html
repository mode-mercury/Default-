Mining & Minting

This open source spirituality chain supports proof-of-work mining for explorers, proof-of-stake minting for token holders, and quest-based minting for ARG players. Bounties reward lore discoveries and community builds.

NFTs, Bounties & Apps

Collect codex pages, archetypes and game items as NFTs. Post bounties for research or code and pay in SpiritToken. Mobile apps connect the blockchain to real-world exploration quests.
